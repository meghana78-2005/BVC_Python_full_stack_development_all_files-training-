I am the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the origin of all that is spiritual and material," Krishna says in the Bhagavad Gita. "Everything emanates from Me, and I am the seat of the impersonal Brahman

I am known as Krishna, the all-attractive One. I am the ParaBrahman (Supreme Self), the cause of all causes, and the original creator, sustainer, and destroyer of the universe.

Born at midnight in Shravan month (July/August) in a prison, Krishna later established the city of Dwaraka, where he reigned as a king.
