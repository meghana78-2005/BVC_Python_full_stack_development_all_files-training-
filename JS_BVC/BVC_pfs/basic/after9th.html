APJ ABDUL KALAM

APJ Abdul Kalam, the former president of India, continues to be an inspiration to many people of all ages and from all backgrounds. His commitment to science and technology and his service to the country earned him the title "Missile Man of the country."

ABOUT

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (About this soundlisten); 15 October 1931 – 27 July 2015, was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.
